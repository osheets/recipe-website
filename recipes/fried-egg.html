Fried Egg

Description

The fried egg is one of the simplest dishes around.

It only takes a few minutes to cook, and a few ingredients to make it truly a delight.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized white or brown egg
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

The Egg

  1. Pour a spoonful of cooking oil on a frying pan on a stove.
  2. Heat the frying pan until sizzling.
  3. Crack the egg against the edge of the frying pan, and slowly pour its contents closely into the pan.
  4. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the egg
  5. Using a spatula, tilt the pan and splash oil onto edges for crispness.
  6. Flip egg if desired.
  7. Let cook to your preferred doneness.
  8. Scoop egg out of the pan

The Rice

  1. Scoop uncooked rice into a rice cooker pot.
  2. Fill the pot with water until level of rice to water surface is the length of the first line of your middle finger.
  3. Cook the rice until done.

Serving

  1. Scoop some cooked rice on to a clean serving plate.
  2. Place cooked egg on top of cooked rice.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
